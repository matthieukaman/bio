# Matthieu Kaman

Development economist and mixed-methods researcher specializing in causal inference, program evaluation, and cost-effectiveness analysis applied to vulnerable populations.

Currently Senior Data Analyst at Homebase and completing MS in International and Development Economics at University of San Francisco.

## Research

**Thesis:** 
- Agency for All, Returns for Some 
— Multi-country RCT examining aspirations and financial literacy interventions across Peru, India, and Uganda. Employed ANCOVA, causal mediation analysis, and causal forests to identify secondary education as the dominant moderator of treatment effectiveness.

**Homelessness Systems Research:**
- Conducted LOTH equity analysis across Cook County, Tulsa, and Santa Clara County identifying heterogeneous pathways to homelessness across racial groups
